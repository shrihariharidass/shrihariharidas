I'm a seasoned FinOps and CloudOps Engineer with extensive experience in cloud cost optimization, financial governance, and infrastructure automation. My work involves automating cost monitoring, implementing governance policies, and providing actionable insights to stakeholders. Beyond my technical role, I'm an active AWS Community Builder, sharing knowledge through technical writing and speaking engagements. I'm passionate about helping organizations navigate their cloud transformation journey while maintaining cost efficiency and operational excellence.
